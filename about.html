About Markuply

Markuply is a playful and ever-growing collection of simple, creative web mini-projects made with just HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It's built for anyone who loves tinkering with code — whether you're just starting out, looking for quick ideas, or need a fun coding break.

How It Started

This project began as a personal experiment. I — Prathewsh S Babu — started building tiny web pages out of boredom and curiosity. One turned into five, five turned into twenty, and soon I realized: this deserves a name, a home, and a place to grow.

There were no big plans, no fancy tech stacks — just raw HTML, pure CSS, and a dash of JavaScript magic. Markuply is a tribute to the joy of building things that just work and make you smile.

What You'll Find Here

Markuply is packed with:

  • 🕹️ Fun games (like Fruit Ninja clones, Snake, Tic-Tac-Toe)
  • 🎨 Creative UI experiments (cards, loaders, animations)
  • 🧠 Mini challenges and logic puzzles in JavaScript
  • 📚 Beginner-friendly code with clean structure

Why It Matters

In a world full of frameworks and boilerplates, sometimes it's refreshing to go back to basics. Markuply helps developers — especially beginners — learn by doing. No setup, no configs, just open the browser and play.

Get Involved

Got a mini idea? Found a bug? Or just want to say hi? Contributions, feedback, and silly ideas are always welcome. You can fork the project, open issues, or suggest features — or simply use it for your own learning!
